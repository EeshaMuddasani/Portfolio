About Me

I have been extremely passionate about art and animation ever since I was young, and over time I have found various ways to refine my craft. Before I entered High School, I learned many drawing techniques from watching videos online and consistently drawing during my free time. When I learned about animation, I was inspired to animate my own creations. I started by using the stop motion feature on my Nintendo DS and my toys in elementary school; by high school, I was creating digital animations. I have created this portfolio to share my passion with others. Check out my instagram and youtube channel to see some of my animations: instagram: eesham27 youtube: Eesha Muddasani.
